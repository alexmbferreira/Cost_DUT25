How collapsing works
  • Car/System/Assembly/Sub-assembly rows have a ▾ caret that collapses their whole block.
  • Part rows have a ▾ caret that collapses only their subparts (process/material/fastener).
  • “Collapse subparts” hides all subrows under every part, without hiding headers.
  • Use the checkbox to show only Warnings/Errors exception chips.
